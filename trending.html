Club 250

Club 250 is the Steam 250 member's club, aggregating all 125 million reviews from Steam to produce full review and ranking history for each game.

Joining Club 250 grants access to more features including our popular Custom Ranking tool, historical charting, automatically marking owned Steam games in rankings, game owners and sales estimates based on GDC research data, ad-free browsing and more. Membership is a Patreon subscription service, but the subscription may be cancelled at any time, even immediately, and you will still enjoy the full term of membership.
