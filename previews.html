Club 250

Club 250 is the Steam 250 member's club, aggregating all 125 million reviews from Steam to produce full review and ranking history for each game.

Joining Club 250 grants access to more site features including our popular Custom Ranking tool, historical charting, automatically marking owned Steam games in rankings, game owners and sales estimates based on real GDC research data, ad-free browsing and more. Check out our membership tiers for the full feature breakdown.

The advocate tier grants access to most site features for just £2 (prices shown in your local currency on sign up). It is a subscription service via Patreon, but if you prefer to just try the service for a month, you may cancel your subscription immediately and still enjoy the full term of membership. You may also start a free trial by linking a free Patreon account and visiting your profile page.
